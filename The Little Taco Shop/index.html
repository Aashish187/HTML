Taco Trivia

When did Tacos first appear in the United States?

Tacos first appeared in the United States in the early 20th century, likely brought by Mexican immigrants. One of the earliest recorded mentions of tacos in the U.S. comes from a newspaper article in 1905, which described Mexican workers introducing the dish.
